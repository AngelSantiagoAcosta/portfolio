Real time crypto data Visualization

This visualization utilizes Alpaca webhooks and a JavaScript library by Trading View called lightweight-charts. First I subscribe to and authenticate the webhook. The data begins streaming in and then I parse the data. I split the data into Quotes, Trades, and Buys, which is then used to populate the chart and tile objects. Another note is that I grab the last hour of historical data to populate the Chart before streaming begins.
